Privacy Policy for Habit Spark

Last updated: 11/08/2023

This Privacy Policy outlines how Habit Spark ("we," "our," or "us") provides information about data practices related to the use of Habit Spark (the "Extension").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of the Extension. The Extension uses the Chrome storage to store habit tracking data locally on the user's device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

Use of Data

The habit tracking data collected by the Extension is solely used for the purpose of providing habit tracking and visualization features within the Extension. The data is stored locally within the user's Chrome browser and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the habit tracking data stored by the Extension. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at manansharm1010@gmail.com so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our data practices. Any changes will be effective upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can contact us at manansharm1010@gmail.com.
